Netflix Movie Reviews

Mean Girls

This movie showed us a lot of things that are relatable, especially for high school students. This movie shows us how some people can be a people pleaser, would do things they dont do, just to please others. They'd do a lot of things just to fit in. All the actors and actresses in this movie had their role that made this movie more interesting. It teaches us a lot of things about life so it's really worth to watch.

Casts

Lindsay Lohan, Rachel McAdams, Amanda Seyfried, Lacey Chabert, Jonathan Bennett, Tina Fey

Review/Rating: 4.5/5

Wild Child

Wild Child is a very entertaining movie, especially for teenagers. It makes us realize the importance of some things and people. It makes us see their real values. We can learn a lot about this movie. Not only for teenagers, but also for parents. The main actress faces a lot of problems and the dad doesn't know that. By that, the parent watchers can maybe realize some things and do better parenting.

Casts

Emma Roberts, Alex Pettyfer, Juno Temple, Natasha Richardson, Kimberley Nixon, Georgia King, Sophie Wu

Review/Rating: 4.6/5

Flipped

The movie Flipped is a very cute movie. It showed teenage love, which is very silly. They were confused with their feelings which explains most of teenagers love. It's somehow relatable so it's really fun to watch.

Casts

Madeline Carroll, Callan McAuliffe, Aidan Quinn, Penelope Ann Miller, John Mahoney, Anthony Edwards

Review/Rating: 4.7/5
